Glorious National Media Team Rules and Regulations

  • He or she must be born again.
  • Must be a worker in his or her church.
  • Must be a Christian with good report.
  • Must be a Christian who minds his or her integrity.
  • Must be ready to obey all the rules and regulations of the department.
  • Must be ready to follow instructions from the leaders.
  • Must be polite and have a teachable heart – ready to learn.
  • Must be ready to abide with the holiness type of dressing we believe in.
  • Must be a disciplined person and someone who takes rebuke when necessary.
  • Must not be someone who is serving in other departments like choir, usher, and technical during the convention.
